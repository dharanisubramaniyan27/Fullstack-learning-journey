Apple

The apple is the round, edible fruit of the Malus domestica tree, a member of the rose family (Rosaceae). Originating in Central Asia, it is one of the most widely cultivated and consumed fruits globally, celebrated for its crisp texture, nutritional density, and impressive health benefits.

Banana

Banana

Bananas are nutrient-dense tropical fruits botanically classified as berries, growing on the world's largest herbaceous plants. Highly valued for their natural sugars, dietary fiber, and high potassium content, they provide quick, sustained energy and support heart health and digestion.

Mango

Mango

The mango (Mangifera indica) is a tropical stone fruit originating from South Asia. Known as the "king of fruits," it is deeply celebrated for its sweet, juicy flesh and vibrant aroma. Cultivated for thousands of years, mangoes are packed with essential vitamins and are enjoyed globally in countless varieties.
